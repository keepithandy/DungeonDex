DungeonDex v1.4.15
Combat Affix Removal & Encounter Simplification Pass

Build query:
1.4.15-combat-affix-removal

Release notes:
- Removed active monster affixes and combat modifier effects for now.
- Removed affix/status labels and popups from combat.
- Simplified encounters into Common, Elite, and Boss tiers.
- Elites and bosses now rely primarily on HP scaling instead of special effects.
- Cleaned combat feed noise caused by status/modifier effects.
- Preserved monster names, families, HP bars, haul, feed, and action buttons.
- Preserved save compatibility with old affix/status data.
- No save wipe.

Scoped notes:
- Old affix, status, and modifier fields may still exist in older saves, but the runtime now ignores them.
- Dormant elite modifier helpers remain available to avoid breaking older save shapes and references.
- Elite Board contracts remain named hunts, but modifier labels are no longer shown or applied.
