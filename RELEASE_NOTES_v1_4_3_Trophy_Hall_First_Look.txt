DungeonDex v1.4.3 — Trophy Hall First Look

Public itch note:
This update gives the old Dex screen its first real identity pass. The section is now the Trophy Hall: a darker collection room for boss trophies first, with space reserved for retired relics in a later pass.

What changed:
- Renamed the Dex navigation tab to Trophy Hall.
- Replaced the old Monster Register / Relic Register presentation with a Boss Trophies tab.
- Added the first 10 boss trophy definitions, covering Boss Floors 5 through 50.
- Added locked and unlocked trophy states.
- Locked boss trophies now appear greyed out until the matching boss milestone has been cleared.
- Added small CSS-built prestige-style trophy icons with skull, horn, drip, and sigil details.
- Added Trophy Hall save support through player.bossTrophies.
- Boss victories now record matching trophy unlocks and log a Trophy Hall message.
- Added a Retired Relics placeholder shelf so the future retired-item system has a natural home.
- Updated cache/build labels to DungeonDex v1.4.3.

Notes:
- The Sootveil Mythic Set from v1.4.2 was already present in this package and was preserved.
- No new 1.4.4 mythic pass was needed yet because the requested mythic set appears to already be implemented.
- This is intentionally a first look: the Trophy Hall can be expanded later with true retired items, richer trophy art, trophy stats, and custom boss identity.
