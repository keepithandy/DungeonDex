DungeonDex v1.4.13 - Elite Board Contract Identity Pass

Overview
- Expands the Elite Board into a three-contract choice system.
- Adds elite names, wanted titles, districts, target floors, threat ratings, modifiers, contract text, bonus writs, reward previews, and flavor.
- Accepted contracts now preserve a real active hunt target that can appear in the run by exact name.
- Contract targets spawn as marked Contract Elites with conservative extra HP, power, and reward scaling.
- Adds accepted-contract visual state, completion/failure/expiration handling, and a compact active-hunt summary.
- Prepares the board for later objective, trophy, and rival-elite passes.

What did not change
- Does not add trophy rewards yet. Trophies are planned for v1.4.15.
- Does not perform broad combat or economy rebalance.
- Does not fully enforce Bonus Writ objectives yet; they are display-only in this pass.
- Does not reset or wipe player saves.
- Does not create a zip or package.
