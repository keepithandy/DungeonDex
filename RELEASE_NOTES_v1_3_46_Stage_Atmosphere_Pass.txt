DungeonDex v1.3.46 — Stage Atmosphere Pass

Scope:
- CSS-only visual pass on the combat monster stage.
- Per-district stage backgrounds and tier atmosphere overlays.
- No combat math, save keys, rewards, scaling, or mechanics changed.

What changed:

Stage backgrounds — per district:
- Each of the 10 districts now has a distinct combat stage background.
- Lowfire (D1–10): warm amber ember hearth light.
- Ashgate (D11–20): cold coal-smoke grey with blue lamplight.
- Ember Debtworks (D21–30): red worklight, crimson chain shadow.
- Sootveil (D31–40): near-dark, black dust, single dim overhead light.
- Cinderbone (D41–50): furnace orange with bone-white highlights.
- Blacktithe (D51–60): gold shadow, sealed amber toll-light.
- Lanternless (D61–70): near-total dark, cold blue void.
- Hunger Kilns (D71–80): open white-hot kiln heat.
- Redwake (D81–90): deep red water-light, bloody mist.
- Final Lowflame (D91–100): ancient ember, vast dark, sacred light from below.

Tier atmosphere overlays:
- Elite stages: purple veil radial overlay on top of district background,
  stronger purple border and outer glow, ground shimmer animation.
- Boss stages: crimson heat radial overlay pressed through the district colour,
  stronger red border and outer glow, ground flare animation.

Panel borders:
- combat-device-shell border tinted per district for subtle colour grounding.

Stage floor animations:
- Boss stage floor: bossGroundFlare keyframe — pulsing ground light.
- Elite stage floor: eliteGroundShimmer keyframe — gentle shimmer.

Base stage:
- Slightly improved base stage background for better atmospheric depth.

Version labels:
- BUILD and VISIBLE_VERSION_LABEL updated to DungeonDex v1.3.46.
- Cache buster strings updated to 1.3.46-stage-atmosphere-pass.

Not changed:
- No combat formulas.
- No enemy scaling.
- No rewards or drop rates.
- No merchant behavior.
- No save format or localStorage keys.
- No new dependencies, npm, or build step.
