DungeonDex v1.4.9a — Town Currency Cleanup

Purpose:
The compact Town currency strip was useful, but Rest and Board costs were redundant inside the Lowfire panel.

Changes:
- Removed Rest cost from the compact Town currency strip.
- Removed Board cost from the compact Town currency strip.
- Kept Coin, Spark, Shards, Ember, and Favor visible as small reference chips.
- Rest price still appears on the Rest action chip.
- Random Board price still appears directly on the Random Board button.
- Added a small overlay script instead of rewriting the larger v1.4.9 talent/board system.

Smoke test:
- Open Town.
- Confirm the top label says DungeonDex v1.4.9a.
- Confirm the small currency strip only shows Coin, Spark, Shards, Ember, and Favor.
- Confirm Rest cost still appears near the Rest button.
- Confirm Random Board still shows its price on the board button.
