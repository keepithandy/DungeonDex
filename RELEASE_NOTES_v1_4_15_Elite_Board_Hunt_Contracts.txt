DungeonDex v1.4.15 - Elite Board Hunt Contracts

Elite Board contracts now create real named hunt targets.
Accepted contracts store a target monster and target floor.
Contract targets appear in-run as HP-scaled Elite Hunts.
Bonus Writs add simple optional objectives and small extra payout.
Combat remains simplified around Common, Elite, Boss, and Board Hunt tiers.
No monster affixes, status effects, or special enemy mechanics were reintroduced.
Save compatibility was preserved.
No save wipe.
