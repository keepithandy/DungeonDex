DungeonDex v1.4.9 — Warden Talents + Lowfire Board Utility Pass

Summary
- Adds the first compact Warden Talent system as a foundation for the road toward 1.5.
- Expands the Lowfire Board with a paid Random Board shuffle.
- Replaces the larger town purse block with a small comparison-focused currency strip.

Warden Talents
- New Gear tab panel: Warden Talents.
- Talent points are earned from level-ups and secured descent progress.
- Three compact trees:
  - Blade: Deep Cut, Quick Footing, Relic Instinct.
  - Ward: Iron Breath, Shield Memory, Lamp Luck.
  - Forge: Scrapper's Eye, Ember Sense, Forge Hand.
- Talent bonuses apply immediately to derived stats, loot chance, shard gains, ember drops, and crafting-adjacent wit.
- Includes a reset button for testing and future tuning.

Lowfire Board
- Adds a Random Board button with a coin price.
- Random Board shuffles visible elite contract offers when no mark is active.
- Active contracts still freeze the board until completed or claimed.
- Board copy was rewritten to explain paid marks in clearer player-facing terms.

Town Currency Strip
- Adds a smaller town strip for quick comparison:
  - Coin
  - Spark
  - Shards
  - Ember
  - Favor
  - Rest cost
  - Board shuffle cost
- Each material has short tooltip copy explaining what it does and how to farm/use it.

Files changed
- index.html

- js/systems/19_warden_talents_lowfire_board.js

Smoke Test Checklist
- Open game and confirm visible label says DungeonDex v1.4.9.
- Open Town and confirm compact currency strip appears below the district text.
- Confirm Random Board button appears inside the Lowfire Elite Board.
- Click Random Board with enough coin and verify coin cost is spent and offers shuffle.
- Accept one elite mark and confirm Random Board no longer offers a shuffle while active.
- Open Gear and confirm Warden Talents panel appears below the player card.
- Spend a talent point on an eligible save and confirm the rank increases and stats update.
- Reset talents and confirm points return.
- Enter combat and verify Attack / Guard / Skill / Extract still function.
