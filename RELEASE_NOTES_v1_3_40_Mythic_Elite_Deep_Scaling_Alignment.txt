DungeonDex v1.3.40 - Mythic/Elite Deep Scaling Alignment

Summary
- Applied a small deep-floor mythic/elite/boss scaling alignment pass based on the v1.3.39 simulator baseline.
- Mythic deep scaling now begins tapering after D500 and reaches the existing deep softener floor by D1,000.
- Elite and boss deep pressure now receives a bounded post-D800 bump so marked fights stay credible around D1,000+.
- Floors 1-40 are intentionally protected; D1 through D500 checkpoint estimates are unchanged by this pass.

Untouched areas
- Merchant stock was intentionally untouched.
- Normal loot, rare loot, legendary loot, drop rates, and mythic set drop chances were intentionally untouched.
- Deep Stair Charter milestone spacing was intentionally not redesigned; simulator wording now calls out the configured post-D800 gap.
- Gold income and early-game rewards were intentionally untouched.
- Save format and localStorage keys were intentionally untouched.
- Mobile layout, CSS, UI systems, and app structure were intentionally untouched.

Simulator reporting
- The deep scaling simulator now separates common enemy, elite enemy, boss enemy, merchant stock, rare loot, legendary loot, mythic estimate, and mythic softener columns.
- Warning language now distinguishes Mythic outruns elite pressure, Mythic outruns boss pressure, Merchant trails enemies, Charter cost high vs common gold, and configured post-D800 charter milestone gaps.

Runtime notes
- Updated internal build/cache labels to v1.3.40.
- The visible player-facing label remains short: DungeonDex v1.3.40.
- No dependencies, npm packages, build step, TypeScript conversion, runtime JSON fetch, app.js split, save migration, or progress reset were added.
