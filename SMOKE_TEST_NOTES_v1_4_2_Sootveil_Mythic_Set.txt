DungeonDex v1.4.2 - Smoke Test Notes

Scope:
- Sootveil Regalia mythic set.
- Sootveil set bonuses.
- Item level cap raised to 3250.
- Version/cache references updated to v1.4.2.

Checks:
1. Confirm title/build label reads DungeonDex v1.4.2.
2. Reach deep loot eligibility, then verify mythic set drops can include Sootveil Crown, Mantle, Vest, Gloves, or Boots.
3. Equip two Sootveil pieces and fight elites until an elite critical occurs. Confirm damage is reduced and compact feedback appears, such as "Sootveil softens the blow."
4. Confirm the 2-piece bonus does not reduce normal elite hits, boss hits, poison, burn, or standard combat damage.
5. Equip three Sootveil pieces and resolve Run Events that grant gold, ember, or shards. Confirm gold is boosted by 15%, ember uses 15% integer reward rounding, and shard events can gain a small extra salvage bump.
6. Confirm Sootveil Run Event salvage does not affect combat rewards, merchant prices, forge output, or elite contract payouts.
7. Equip five Sootveil pieces and take lethal combat strike damage. Confirm Sootveil Escape leaves the Warden at exactly 1 HP, adds a small Guard buffer, and logs compact feedback.
8. In the same run, take another lethal strike. Confirm Sootveil Escape does not trigger again.
9. Start a new run. Confirm Sootveil Escape is reset and can trigger once again.
10. Generate or load gear above ilvl 999. Confirm ilvl values above 999 persist through save/load and display correctly in inventory/loadout cards.
11. Confirm generated gear does not exceed ilvl 3250.
12. Check combat still supports attack, skill, guard, extract, loot drops, elite modifiers, boss rewards, and Run Events without regression.
13. Save, reload, equip Sootveil pieces, and verify no save corruption or set recognition loss.

Notes:
- Sootveil shard and ember improvements are chance/rounding based because those rewards are integer currencies.
- This pass intentionally does not add currencies, tabs, modal flows, dodge/evasion stats, or inventory redesigns.
